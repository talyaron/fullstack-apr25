Hitomi has a secret crush on his kind and muscular neighbor, Kamiya. Hitomi always brings him extra food from his part-time job, but, one day, Kamiya's attitude suddenly changes. His kind smile disappears and with wild panting, he steals a kiss from Hitomi while pushing him against the wall. Then, animal ears appear on Kamiya's head...! This is a passionate love story between a noble college student and a gentle giant who suffers from theriomorphosis.
