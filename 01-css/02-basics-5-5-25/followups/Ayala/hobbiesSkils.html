Western Horse Riding

Western horse riding connects me to the grace and power of these magnificent animals. I enjoy the partnership and trust built with the horse, the feeling of open spaces, and the skill involved in guiding them through different maneuvers.

Art & Painting

Art and painting allow me to translate the world I see and feel onto a canvas. I love the freedom of expression, the way colors blend and create emotions, and the satisfaction of bringing a vision to life through my own hands.

Fitness & Boxing

Fitness and boxing provide a fantastic outlet for both physical and mental energy. I appreciate the discipline and strength training involved, the feeling of empowerment after a good workout, and the focus required in boxing. .

Cooking

Cooking is a creative and nurturing process that I find incredibly rewarding. I love experimenting with flavors, learning new techniques, and the joy of sharing delicious food that brings people together.

Nature

Being in nature is where I find peace and inspiration. I am drawn to the beauty and complexity of the natural world, from vast landscapes to tiny details, and I enjoy exploring, observing, and feeling connected to something larger than myself.
