An Academic Journey: From Psychology to Statistics

Academically, I've always been driven by a desire to understand the human mind and the logic of numbers. This led me to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Psychology, which gave me fascinating insights into human behavior. My intellectual curiosity didn't stop there, and I went on to earn a Master's degree in Statistics. This combination of understanding both people and data has proven to be incredibly valuable in my professional life.

Career in Quality Engineering in the Medical Industry
