When do we finish studying? At the end of high school?
Upon graduation? And maybe never...?

The digital learning revolution - the best courses, for everyone, for free.

The dynamic and changing world we live in requires each and every one of us to learn throughout life. The good news is that the technological developments of our time make it possible to learn from anywhere, at any time and at the appropriate pace.

The headquarters of the National Digital Israel Initiative in the National Digital System and the Council for Higher Education joined a joint strategic initiative in order to enable every person in Israel to acquire an education and develop skills for their academic, professional and personal advancement.

Campus-IL, the national initiative for digital learning, is based on the unique platform from edX, developed by Harvard and MIT universities, and enables wide audiences to develop and advance throughout life - from high school students and students, through teachers, civil servants and job seekers, to senior citizens And every curious person who wants to expand their horizons.

Here you will find the best digital courses from the best academic institutions, government ministries and other public bodies in Israel. The courses, which were produced at a very high level and with an emphasis on the quality of the teaching, combine videos, advanced interactive tools, forums and a variety of experiential and personalized learning paths.

We wish you a fun and meaningful learning!
