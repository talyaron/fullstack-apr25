Me

Hello, my name is Yatir Yehuda. I am 35 years old, married to Reut and the father of 3 daughters: Neama Liri and Yahav A little more information about myself: I have been living in Yeruham for 10 years, but I was born and raised in a moshav in the northern Negev called "Bitha."

My Family

There are 5 of us in the family: me, my wife Reut - 33 years old Neama - 9 years old Liri - 6 years old And Yahav - 4 years old And in this section I will tell you a little about my family. Reut and I have known each other for almost 14 years and have been married for a little over 10 years (2014), and during this time we have had 3 daughters. Neama (2015), Liri (2018) and Yahav (2021). Reut works in a kindergarten Neama is in 4th grade Liri is in 1st grade Yahav is in kindergarten. We live in a quiet neighborhood in Yeruham (Central Negev - half an hour south of Be'er Sheva).

My History

I was born 35 years ago in Moshav Bitha and lived there until I was 25. I joined the army in 2007 and served in the Intelligence Corps. After 3 years, I finished my duty and became a citizen for a period of 4 years, at the end of which I returned to military service for 7 years in the Intelligence Corps until 2022. In 2022, I started working for a company called “Raval” and I am responsible for exporting its goods abroad, mainly to the US, Luxembourg and China

My Present

I currently work for a company called "Raval" and responsible for exporting its goods abroad. In addition, I have been serving in the reserves since October 7th, each time for a period of several months. And for this time, it,s the third round.
