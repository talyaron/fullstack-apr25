Who I Am

My name is Zina Biton, and I am a strong and inspiring woman.Half Israeli and half Bedouin, I grew up in a Moroccan home with nine siblings,surrounded by love and warmth. My life journey began at the age of 17when I received my first gift from God—my eldest daughter. From that moment,I knew my life would be dedicated to her. Eight years later,another unexpected surprise arrived—three more gifts I had not been prepared for,yet they became the driving force of my life. Today, at 35, I am proud to present myself as a successful woman, one who overcomes challenges and leads her life with strength, faith and success

My Hobbies
